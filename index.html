Information

Welcome to the home page of all of Chia's websites. There are so many more websites to explore.

There are many things I love and many things I might be.

I love to put my life on the internet because there is no where else where I can truly live. I love to be here because it's where all the people I love are, where I could potentially gather them all in one place. I love resistance, memories, sounds, and things. I love the web because it is the place where I can preserve myself and for others to explore me too, but I have the agency to control what you see and what you don't see. I love the web because I can disappear from it (somewhat) at any moment. I love the web for its poetics, sublimity, and intransigence. I love letters on websites and think more websites should start with declarations, love letters, poems, and maps. I love every form of media and archive, I love the internet for its indexes and dead ends and its space and it being the space that has allowed me to be here and become.

Currently in New Haven, Connecticut.
Born and raised in Manila, Philippines.
